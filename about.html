Our Story

Yummy Cakes was born from a simple idea: “Delicious cakes made with care and creativity.”

Our founder, Joanita, began baking for family and friends, perfecting recipes late into the night and experimenting with flavors that turned simple moments into unforgettable celebrations.

Word spread, orders grew, and soon Yummy Cakes became a beloved name known for flavor, freshness, and artistic flair.

Today, we proudly serve customers who trust us to make their special days sweeter from birthdays and weddings to baby showers and corporate events.

Our Promise

Every cake that leaves our kitchen is made from scratch, using only premium, high-quality ingredients never pre-mixed or frozen.

We use real butter, farm-fresh eggs, pure vanilla, and the finest chocolate to ensure every bite melts in your mouth and leaves you wanting more.

Each design is handcrafted with care and attention to detail, so your cake doesnot just taste amazing it looks stunning, too.

Whether you want something elegant and classic or fun and playful, we will make it exactly how you imagine it.
